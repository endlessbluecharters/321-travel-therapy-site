Meet your advisor

Travel isn't always pretty. I know — I've lived it.

For some of us, travel comes with sweaty palms at the gate, three checked bags, a carry-on full of "just in case," a pill organizer the size of a small country, and a search history that includes "what if the plane" more times than we'd like to admit. If that sounds familiar — welcome. You are exactly who I built this for.

321 Travel Therapy was born out of my own complicated relationship with travel. I'm an anxious, quirky, high-maintenance traveler who has been to all corners of the globe anyway — because the world is too extraordinary to let fear, logistics, or a sensitive stomach keep you home.

After years of homeschooling my daughter around the globe, travel became my therapy. I figured out how to do it beautifully — with real accommodations, careful planning, and the right people in my corner. Now I do that for you.

"I've been that traveler. I am that traveler. And I will make sure you get there — comfortably, confidently, and with a lot less panic than you started with."

— Lynn Richards-Suley, Certified Travel Advisor
